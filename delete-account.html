Delete Your Flowstone Account

How to delete your Flowstone account and the data associated with it.

Flowstone is operated by The Nest Investment Group LLC. You can delete your account and the personal data tied to it at any time, using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete it yourself in the app (immediate)

  1. Open Flowstone and go to your own (owner) profile.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Go to Danger zone › Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and its data are deleted immediately.

Parents can delete a child's profile the same way, from Settings › Danger zone › Delete account.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, or would rather we do it, email inquiry@thenestinvestmentgroup.com from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We respond to deletion requests within 30 days and may ask reasonable questions to confirm the request is genuinely yours.

Delete only some of your data (without closing your account)

You do not have to delete your whole account to remove data:

  • Journal entries can be deleted individually in the app: open Journal, open the entry, and choose Delete.
  • To have us remove other specific data, email inquiry@thenestinvestmentgroup.com from your account's email address and tell us what you would like deleted. We respond within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account removes the personal data tied to it, including your check-ins, sessions, intentions, mood history, journal entries, goal and reminder preferences, and any sleep records synced from Apple Health or Health Connect.

Timing and what we may keep

  • In-app deletion is immediate.
  • Data stored on our servers is deleted within 60 days of account deletion, except where we must keep a copy to comply with the law or resolve a dispute.
  • Operational/security logs are kept for a short period (typically 30–90 days) and then aggregated or deleted.

For full details on how Flowstone handles your data, see our Privacy Policy.
